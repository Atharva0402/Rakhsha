Community Guidelines

Our commitment at Raksha to provide a secure and compassionate environment for women. These guidelines form the core of our community, emphasizing empathy, respect, and kindness in all interactions. Harassment, discrimination, and bullying are strictly prohibited, fostering a supportive atmosphere. We prioritize privacy and urge users to respect confidentiality. Abuse of any kind is not tolerated, with a zero-tolerance policy. Inclusivity and diversity are celebrated, ensuring everyone feels valued. Compliance with laws and responsible reporting is essential, while authenticity and responsible content sharing maintain a safe space. In emergencies, contact local authorities for assistance. Thank you for being part of our community dedicated to women's safety and empowerment.
